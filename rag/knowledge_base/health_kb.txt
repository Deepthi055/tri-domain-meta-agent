DOCUMENT: Healthy Lifestyle Guide
SOURCE: TriDomain Health KB v1.0

Good health depends on proper nutrition, regular exercise, quality sleep, stress management, and preventive healthcare.

Healthy habits developed gradually are more sustainable than drastic lifestyle changes.

---

DOCUMENT: BMI Classification
SOURCE: TriDomain Health KB v1.0

Body Mass Index (BMI)

BMI =
Weight (kg) / Height² (m²)

BMI Categories:

Below 18.5
Underweight

18.5–24.9
Healthy Weight

25–29.9
Overweight

30+
Obese

BMI is only a screening tool and should not replace professional medical advice.

---

DOCUMENT: Exercise Recommendations
SOURCE: TriDomain Health KB v1.0

WHO recommends:

150–300 minutes of moderate exercise weekly

OR

75–150 minutes of vigorous exercise weekly.

Include:
- Cardio
- Strength Training
- Flexibility
- Balance exercises

Exercise improves heart health, mental well-being, and weight management.

Complex carbohydrates

Healthy fats

Fruits

Vegetables

Whole grains

Limit:
Sugar
Processed foods
Trans fats
Excess sodium

---

DOCUMENT: Sleep Health
SOURCE: TriDomain Health KB v1.0

Adults should sleep 7–9 hours daily.

Good sleep improves:

Memory

Concentration

Immunity

Hormone regulation

Mental health

Poor sleep increases risks of obesity, diabetes, hypertension, and depression.

---

DOCUMENT: Mental Wellness
SOURCE: TriDomain Health KB v1.0

Mental health is equally important as physical health.

Healthy practices:

Meditation

Deep breathing

Exercise

Social support

Journaling

Seeking professional help when necessary

Persistent anxiety or depression should be evaluated by qualified healthcare professionals.

---

DOCUMENT: Stress Management
SOURCE: TriDomain Health KB v1.0

Stress can affect physical and emotional well-being.

Stress reduction techniques:

Regular exercise

Time management

Mindfulness

Adequate sleep

Healthy diet

Hobbies

Avoid chronic stress whenever possible.

---

DOCUMENT: Weight Management
SOURCE: TriDomain Health KB v1.0

Healthy weight loss should be gradual.

Recommended:

0.5–1 kg per week

Avoid:

Crash diets

Extreme calorie restriction

Unverified supplements

Sustainable lifestyle changes are more effective.

DOCUMENT: Common Health Indicators
SOURCE: TriDomain Health KB v1.0

Healthy Resting Heart Rate:
60–100 bpm

Healthy Blood Pressure:
Around 120/80 mmHg

Normal Fasting Blood Sugar:
70–99 mg/dL

Consult healthcare professionals for proper interpretation.

DOCUMENT: Health Myths to Avoid
SOURCE: TriDomain Health KB v1.0

Common misconceptions:

Skipping meals does not guarantee weight loss.

Supplements cannot replace a balanced diet.

Spot reduction of body fat is not scientifically supported.

More exercise is not always better.

Rapid weight loss is usually unsustainable.

Health decisions should be based on scientific evidence and professional medical advice.
